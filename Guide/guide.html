Citizens (Part 1)

The citizens will have one of four base states:

Citizens who have not been infected by the virus will be shown as a plain circle.

Citizens who are infected by the virus will be shown as a green virus.

Citizens who were infected by the virus, but have recovered will be shown as a plain circle with green arms.

Citizens who have died because of the virus will be shown as a skull.

Citizens (Part 2)

If a citizen is following a certain policy, then their base icon will have one of the following effects over their icon.

*If the citizen is following multiple policies, then the policy that better protects them from the virus will be displayed. The policies are listed in order of effectiveness.

Citizens who have been vaccinated will have a blue aura around their base icon.

Citizens who are wearing a mask will be a shown with a mask over their base icon.

Citizens who are social distancing, will have an arrow pointing in both directions over their icon.
