Intro

Epidemics and pandemics are not going anywhere and will forever be a part of history. Mask-It-Or-Casket was created to help visualize disease spread and the impact of policy implementations on it. Aspects of the game were included to make the game more realistic - money and time is needed for policies, vaccines need to be developed before they can be used, the risk bar was based off a model the New York Times used to assess transmission risk. We hope Mask-It-Or-Casket will give you some insight into some of the work that goes into fighting against epidemics and pandemics!


How to Win

Strategize and implement policies within your budget and in time.
Keep the risk as low as possible so you can gain more green points and reduce your red points.

Long story short: Protect the people from the disease in time to save as many people as you can!
